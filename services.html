Freeview

Freeview services consists of high definition capable digital terrestrial television, Freeview|HD, and standard definition, digital satellite television. We provide the following services for Freeview setups:
New UHF Aerial installation, VHF To UHF Aerial Upgrade, Signal Repair.

Satellite TV

The benefit of satellite TV is it works anywhere in New Zealand since the TV signals are from satellites in space and you have a lot of free-to-air TV channels that you can access to from any satellite of your choice. The services we provide are:
New Satellite Dish Installation, Satellite Signal Repair, Sky TV Services.

Home Setups

If you have just bought a Television but DIY is not your thing in terms of technology, we can connect your TV and set it up for you. We will show you how to operate it and we will keep it simple. The services we provide are:
TV Cabling, Data Cabling, HDMI And Remote Extender, TV Wall Mounting, Home Theater Setups.

Business Buildings

What sets us apart is that we also provide commercial building TV networking. Though the aerial and dish used are the same for both commercial and residential, the infrustructure is different. For new installation, prices are made with an onsite quotation so please contact us.
